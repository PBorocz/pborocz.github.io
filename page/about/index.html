Site Name/Tag Line

Many (many) years ago, my hour-long daily commute time was taken up with CNBC’s morning market coverage (specifically SquawkAlley). When markets were essentially unchanged (or flat in the vernacular), Bertha Coombs opined once that markets were “flirting with neutrality”.

I noted this wonderful aphorism at the time and it came back to me in the current climate. Specifically, my predilection to holding socially liberal but economically conservative views. Thus, I don’t easily fit anywhere on the classic Republican-Democratic spectrum (albeit leaning heavily small and large “D” democratic for the majority of policy issues).

Thus, Flirting with Neutrality it is!
